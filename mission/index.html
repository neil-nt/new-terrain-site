About New Terrain

AI won't transform your business.

The landscape

The same AI. Different results.

Every business now has access to the same AI. The same models, the same tools, the same capabilities. Used only for speed, it makes you faster at work that looks like everyone else's. The advantage isn't access. It's what you do with what you uniquely know.

We help businesses use AI to build on their expertise, not around it. That means getting honest about how the business really works, making your knowledge something the whole business can build on, and designing the change so it sticks and pays back.

Our approach

How we work.

Three things guide everything we do. AI accelerates what's already there, so the foundations have to be right. Change doesn't last unless your people change with it. And efficiency only matters if it funds better work: margin recovery is a moment, growth is a model.

Our clients

Who we work with.

We work with growth businesses where expertise is the business, led by people with a clear view of where they need to get to as AI reshapes their market. We build alongside them, and we build for their independence, not ours.
